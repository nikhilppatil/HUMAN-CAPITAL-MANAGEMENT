**Modules in ZOHO People:**



**\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_**





1. **Employee Database Management**

   Central repository for all employee information.
   
2. **Leave Tracker Module**

   Manage and track employee leave requests and balances.
   
3. **Attendance Management**

   Monitor employee attendance and timings.
   
4. **Shifts Module**

   Configure and manage employee shifts and schedules.
   
5. **Time Tracker Module**

   Track employee working hours and time spent on tasks.
   
6. **Onboarding Module**

   Includes separate forms for Candidates and Employees to streamline hiring and induction processes.
   
7. **File Module**

   File storage with configured restrictions based on IP and Geo-location for secure access.
   
8. **Cases Module**

   Centralized help desk where employees can raise HR-related tickets and issues.
   
9. **Employee Engagement Module**

   Tools to enhance employee satisfaction through feedback, surveys, and announcements.
   
10. **Tasks Module**

    Task assignment and tracking for employees.
    
11. **Performance Module**

    Conduct appraisals and performance reviews.
    
12. **Compensation Module**

    Manage employee compensation and payroll-related details.
    
13. **LMS (Learning Management System)**


&nbsp;  Training and development module for employee learning.
