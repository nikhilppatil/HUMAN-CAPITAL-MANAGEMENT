* Gross Salary = Basic + HRA + Allowances + Bonus : Earnings before any deductions.



* Deductions = PF + ESI + PT + TDS + Others



* Net Salary = Gross Salary – Deductions : In Hand salary after deductions.



* CTC = Gross Salary + Employer PF + Employer ESI + Gratuity + Benefits



**==============================================================================================================================**



**1. Gross Salary**



**Definition:** The total earnings of an employee before any deductions.



**Formula:**



**Gross Salary = Basic + HRA + Allowances + Bonus**



**Components:**



**Basic Salary:**

The fixed part of your salary. It usually forms 35% to 50% of your CTC.

Taxable component.



**HRA (House Rent Allowance):**

Given to employees living in rented accommodation.

Partially exempt under Section 10(13A) of the Income Tax Act.



**Allowances (can vary by company):**



Conveyance Allowance – for travel to work



Medical Allowance – for medical expenses



Special Allowance – fully taxable, used to balance CTC



Leave Travel Allowance (LTA) – for travel within India (tax-exempt under conditions)



**Bonus:**

Performance-based or statutory bonus paid annually or monthly.

Taxable.

**2. Deductions**



**Definition:** Amounts deducted from Gross Salary to arrive at Net Salary.



**Formula:**



**Deductions = PF + ESI + PT + TDS + Others**



**Common Deductions:**



**PF (Provident Fund):**

Employee contributes 12% of Basic Salary.

Tax-exempt under Section 80C.



**ESI (Employees’ State Insurance):**

Applicable if Gross Salary ≤ ₹21,000/month. Employee contributes 0.75%.

(Only applicable to eligible employees.)



**PT (Professional Tax):**

State-specific, varies from ₹200 to ₹2,500/year. Not all states apply it.



**TDS (Tax Deducted at Source):**

Based on your total income and tax slab. Paid to government on your behalf.



**Other Deductions:**



Salary advances



Loan EMIs



Meal coupons



Insurance premiums (if deducted)

**3. Net Salary (Take-Home Salary)**



**Definition:** The amount credited to your bank account after all deductions.



**Formula:**



**Net Salary = Gross Salary – Deductions**

**4. CTC (Cost to Company)**



**Definition:** The total amount a company spends on an employee annually, including both direct and indirect benefits.



**Formula:**



**CTC = Gross Salary + Employer PF + Employer ESI + Gratuity + Benefits**



**Additional Components in CTC:**



**Employer PF Contribution:**

12% of Basic (not part of Net Salary).



**Employer ESI Contribution:**

3.25% of Gross Salary (if applicable).



**Gratuity:**

Applicable if you complete 5 years. Formula:

Gratuity = (15/26) × Last Drawn Basic × No. of years

Usually ~4.81% of Basic included in CTC.



**Benefits (variable and non-cash):**



Medical Insurance (group health policy)



Meal Coupons (e.g., Sodexo, Zeta)



Company Car / Fuel Allowance



Mobile Reimbursement



Stock Options (ESOPs)



Joining Bonus / Retention Bonus



Training \& Development Costs
