#### **Case Management Flowchart** 



##### **1. Master Data Setup (Admin Configuration)**



**Admin Configures Masters:**



1. Common Masters (Employees, Departments, Colleges/Institutes/Organization)
   
2. Case Department
   
3. Case Name
   
4. Document Master
   
5. Case Document Mapping
   
6. Committee Master
   
7. Committee Designation
   
8. Committee Member Mapping
   
9. Case Approval Path
   
10. Case Category



**→ Output:** Master data ready for case processing.



**=============================================================================================================================================**



##### **2. Case Transaction Flow**



1. **Case Creation**



Employee/Department initiates a case.



Assign case category, department, and related documents.



**2. Case Receive**



Case received by the system/HR/Admin.



Automatic logging with timestamp.



**3. Case Role Allocation**



Allocate case to the relevant committee/authority based on Approval Path.



Assign roles: Reviewer, Approver, Manager.



**4. Resolution Upload**



Committee/Authority uploads resolution, remarks, and supporting documents.



**5. Case Disposal**



Final approval and case closure.



Update status in the system.



**6. View Resolution**



Authorized users can view the case resolution and documents.



**====================================================================================================================================================**
